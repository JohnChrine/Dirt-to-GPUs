Privacy

Simple version: your email stays with this list.

If you join From Dirt to GPUs, your email is used for the field notes list and direct follow-up connected to this project. It is not sold, rented, or handed around.

The current local v1 stores subscriber emails, signup source, admin notes, local DM drafts, and basic event history so the list can be managed responsibly. Before a public launch, this will be connected to a real hosting and email setup.

Want out later? Reply to a message or ask to be removed, and you will be removed.
