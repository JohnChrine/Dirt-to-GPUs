# From Dirt to GPUs Launch Checklist

Public-safe launch reminders:

- Keep the site voice clear, direct, and useful.
- Keep signup language simple and transparent.
- Test every published field note on desktop and mobile.
- Test every subscriber email before sending it to the list.
- Keep private operational notes, credentials, subscriber data, and backups out
  of the repository.
- Avoid employer names, confidential project details, client names, site
  locations, non-public timelines, or anything learned under NDA.
