# From Dirt to GPUs Voice Notes

## Core line

Field notes from a guy who likes building data centers.

## Current site promise

A fresh perspective on the people, projects, power, and career paths behind the data centers powering AI.

## Tone

- Raw but not reckless.
- Young but not insecure.
- Field-side, not fake executive.
- Curious, direct, relationship-driven.
- No fake expertise. No employer gossip. No confidential details.
- Say the quiet part clearly.

## Words that fit

- Dirt
- Power
- Schedule
- Rack ready
- Field notes
- Buildout
- Nerve
- Relationships
- Learning fast
- Execution
- Messy middle

## Words to be careful with

- Confidence, if it sounds like self-help.
- Thought leader.
- Insider, if it implies confidential information.
- Guru, expert, masterclass.
- Passive income, on public-facing pages.

## Lines we like

- No bullshit. Just raw data center construction experience.
- AI has a dirt phase.
- Before the keynote, there is a schedule.
- Before the model, there is power.
- I am not the gray-haired expert. That is kind of the point.

## First Field Notes ideas

- AI does not start with a model.
- Rack ready is not a vibe.
- The room with more experience does not scare me anymore.
- Power is the new gold rush, but schedule is still the tax.
- GC side, owner side, tenant side: what changes when the chair changes.
- Relationships are infrastructure too.
- The AI boom has a labor problem nobody makes sexy.
